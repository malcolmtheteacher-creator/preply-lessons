Speaking Practice

Discussion Question 1

What do you do with your friends?
Think about your activities with friends.
Examples: "I play video games with my friends." / "I go shopping with my friends."

Discussion Question 2

What do you do with your family?
Think about family activities, especially on weekends.

Discussion Question 3

What do you prefer to do alone?
Think about activities you like doing by yourself.

Discussion Question 4

What do you do with your classmates?
Think about school activities and after-school activities.

Discussion Question 5

Do you prefer doing things alone or with other people? Why?
Explain your preference and give examples.
Now tell your teacher about 5 different activities and who you do them with!

Deeper Discussion

Personal Question 1

Who is your best friend? What do you usually do together?
Talk about your best friend and your activities together.

Personal Question 2

What activities do you do with your family on the weekend?
Describe a typical weekend with your family.

Personal Question 3

Tell me about something you like to do alone. Why do you prefer doing it alone?
Explain an activity and why you like doing it by yourself.
