SAYING TIMES
7:00 = seven o'clock / seven AM
7:30 = seven thirty / half past seven
7:15 = seven fifteen / quarter past seven
12:00 = twelve o'clock / noon / midday
19:00 = seven PM / seven o'clock (evening)

AM vs PM

AM = morning (midnight to noon)
PM = afternoon and evening (noon to midnight)
Examples:
‚Ä¢ 7:00 AM = 7 o'clock in the morning üåÖ

Speaking Practice

Discussion Question 1

Describe your morning routine.
Talk about what you do from when you wake up until you go to school.
Example: "I wake up at 7:00 AM. I brush my teeth and eat breakfast. Then I go to school at 8:00 AM."

Discussion Question 2

What do you do after school?
Describe your afternoon routine.
Example: "After school, I do homework at 4:00 PM. Then I play video games."

Discussion Question 3

What is your evening routine?
Talk about what you do in the evening before bed.

Discussion Question 4

What time do you usually do your favorite activity?
Talk about your hobbies and when you do them.
