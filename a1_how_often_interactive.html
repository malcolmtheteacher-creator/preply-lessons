Speaking Practice

Discussion Question 1

What do you always do every day?
Think about your daily routine. Examples:
‚Ä¢ "I always brush my teeth in the morning."
‚Ä¢ "I always check my phone when I wake up."

Discussion Question 2

What do you usually do in your free time?
Think about what you do most days after school or on weekends.

Discussion Question 3

What do you sometimes do on weekends?
Think about activities you do occasionally, not every weekend.

Discussion Question 4

What do you never do?
Think about things you don't do at all. Example:
‚Ä¢ "I never wake up early on weekends."

Discussion Question 5

Challenge Questions

1. What activities do you do every single day?
Make a list and explain: "I always..."
2. What's something you used to do often, but now you never do?
Think about how your habits have changed.
3. What would you like to do more often?
Example: "I sometimes exercise, but I want to do it more often."
