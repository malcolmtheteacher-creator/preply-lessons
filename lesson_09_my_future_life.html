üìö Key Vocabulary Review:

achievement
Something important that you succeed in doing
"Graduating from university was my biggest achievement."
goal
Something you want to achieve in the future
"My goal is to speak five languages fluently."
prediction
A statement about what you think will happen
"My prediction is that cars will be electric in 20 years."
bucket list
A list of things you want to do before you die
"Visiting Japan is on my bucket list!"
settle down
To start living a stable, calm life (usually with family)
"I want to travel for a few years, then settle down."
life stage
A particular period in someone's life
"University is an important life stage."
long-term
Continuing for a long time into the future
"What are your long-term career plans?"
aspiration
A strong desire to achieve something; an ambition
"Her aspiration is to become a doctor."
