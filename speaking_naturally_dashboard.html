Speaking Naturally

Master Natural English Conversation

A 6-Part Interactive Course Series | B2-C1 Level

About This Course

Speaking Naturally is designed for advanced learners (B2-C1) who want to sound more natural and confident in English conversations. Each lesson focuses on a specific aspect of spoken English with focused vocabulary (5-6 expressions per lesson), topic card selection for personalized practice, and timed speaking activities to build fluency.

Course Level

B2-C1 (Upper Intermediate to Advanced)

Lessons

6 interactive lessons, 50 minutes each

Focus

Speaking fluency, natural expressions, conversation skills

Method

Interactive activities, timed debates, topic cards
