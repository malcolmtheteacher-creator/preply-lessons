Speaking Practice

Discussion 1

What are 3 things you CAN do?
Tell your teacher 3 abilities or skills you have!
Example: "I can swim, I can speak French, and I can play video games!"

Discussion 2

What are 3 things you CAN'T do?
Tell your teacher 3 things you can't do.
Example: "I can't drive a car, I can't cook, and I can't speak Spanish."

Discussion 3

What CAN'T you do now, but you WANT to learn?
Think about skills you want to have in the future!
Example: "I can't cook now, but I want to learn!"

Discussion 4

What can you do VERY WELL?
Talk about your best skills!
Example: "I can speak French very well!"
Or: "I can play video games very well!"
